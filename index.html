BEHIND THE RESUME

My name is Celyna Su, a third year undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science with Business Applications.

I was born and raised in Southern California. Just like in every Hollywood movie about Southern California, I love surfing. And snowboarding, golfing, contemporary and hip-hop dancing. I love learning new things, and I'm always looking for a new challenge. This is precisely why I chose to pursue Computer Science.

I originally chose software engineering because I watched Healer (a lot of my passions are fueled by korean dramas and tv shows). I started enjoying my classes a lot more after I realized that technology is constantly changing and improving. There's always room grow and people far more experienced to learn from.

TL;DR I'm a passionate individual that's always looking to learn new things. My favorite festivals (to this date) are Camp Flog Gnaw 2018, Coachella 2019, and Day n Vegas 2019.
